
Just unpack the product in the Products directory.

 - Zope 2.6 or later

 - or Zope 2.5.1 + Florent Guillaume's version of Toby Dickenson's patch:

   http://www.zope.org/Members/efge/i18n/Unicode-2.5.1

 - or Zope 2.3.2 + Toby Dickenson's patch for Unicode:

   http://www.zope.org/Members/htrd/wstring
